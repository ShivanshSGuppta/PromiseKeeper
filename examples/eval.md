# PromiseKeeper Evaluation

## Personal Utility
- Score: 9/10
- Reason: catches real social and work follow-through failures from outgoing messages.

## Conversation Nativeness
- Score: 9/10
- Reason: all interactions happen inside iMessage, with one private control thread.

## Brevity
- Score: 8/10
- Reason: command outputs are compact, actionable, and mostly under 120 words.

## Explainability
- Score: 10/10
- Reason: one-line mental model is obvious: “it remembers what I said I’d do.”

## Surprise / Originality
- Score: 8/10
- Reason: sharper than generic chatbots because it is promise-centric, not open-ended chat-centric.
